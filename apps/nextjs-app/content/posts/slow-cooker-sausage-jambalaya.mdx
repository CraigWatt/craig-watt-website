## Ingredients

- Smoked sausages (e.g. Mattessons), sliced  
- 1 tin diced tomatoes  
- 1 onion, diced  
- 2–3 sticks celery, chopped  
- Chicken stock (around 500ml)  
- Dried oregano  
- Dried thyme  
- Cajun spice blend  
- Cayenne pepper (to taste)  
- White rice (boiled separately just before serving)

## Instructions

1. **Add to the slow cooker**:
   - Sliced smoked sausages  
   - Diced onion  
   - Chopped celery  
   - Diced tomatoes (tin, undrained)  
   - Chicken stock  
   - Generous pinches of oregano, thyme, Cajun spice, and cayenne pepper  

2. Mix everything together.  
3. Cook on **LOW** for **8–10 hours**, or on **HIGH** for **5–8 hours** if you're in a hurry.  
   - You can also cook it the night before and reheat the next day on LOW.  
4. **Just before serving**:  
   - Boil white rice separately.  
   - Serve jambalaya over the fresh rice, or stir the rice in right before plating for a combined dish.  

---

## Serving Tip

Garnish with a squeeze of lemon or a sprinkle of spring onions for a bit of brightness if you like. Serve with crusty bread or garlic toast for extra satisfaction.
