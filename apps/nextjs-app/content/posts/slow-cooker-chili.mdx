## Ingredients

- 1 pack of mince  
- 1 jar of ground cumin powder  
- 2 tins chopped tomatoes  
- 1 tin of kidney beans (use half the tin)  
- Mushrooms (a handful, chopped)  
- 1 onion (chopped)  
- 1 tube of tomato purée (use half the tube)  
- 1 packet of red or mixed red/green chillies  
- 3 beef OXO cubes  
- 1 ham cube (optional)  
- Rice (to serve)  
- Tortilla chips or Doritos (to serve)  

## Instructions

1. Place the mince into the slow cooker.  
2. Chop the onion and mushrooms, then add to the slow cooker and stir.  
3. Let them cook down until water is released. Sieve the liquid out, then return the contents to the slow cooker.  
4. Add 3 beef OXO cubes and stir well.  
5. Add 3 heaped teaspoons of cumin powder and stir.  
6. Add 1 teaspoon of mild chilli powder and stir.  
7. Drain the kidney beans and add **half** the tin to the slow cooker.  
8. Add half the tube of tomato purée.  
9. Add 1 tin of chopped tomatoes and stir everything together.  
10. Let it cook low and slow until the flavours meld (at least 4–6 hours).  

## Serving Suggestions

Serve with fluffy rice and a side of tortilla chips or Doritos for crunch.  
Add sour cream or grated cheese if you’re feeling extra fancy.
