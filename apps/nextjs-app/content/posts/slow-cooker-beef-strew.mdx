## Ingredients

- Puff pastry (ready-rolled sheet)  
- 4–5 potatoes  
- Butter (for mash)  
- Milk (for mash)  
- 1 onion  
- 2–3 carrots  
- 1 portion of beef stew meat  
- 3 beef OXO cubes  
- Bisto gravy granules  
- Boiling water  

## Instructions

1. Boil the kettle.  
2. Place the beef stew meat into the slow cooker.  
3. Crumble in **1 beef OXO cube**.  
4. Peel and chop the **potatoes**, then place them in a pot of cold water (for mash later).  
5. Peel and chop the **onion**, then add to the slow cooker.  
6. Crumble in the **2nd beef OXO cube**.  
7. Peel and chop the **carrots**, then add to the slow cooker.  
8. Crumble in the **3rd beef OXO cube**.  
9. Pour in the freshly boiled water — enough to fill around **½ to ⅗** of the slow cooker.  
10. Give it a mix (if possible) and set the slow cooker to **LOW**.  
11. Let it simmer on LOW **overnight** and throughout the next day until **tea time**, stirring 2–3 times throughout.  

---

## Serving Instructions

1. **20 minutes before serving**:  
   - Take the puff pastry out of the fridge.  
   - Preheat the oven.  
2. **15 minutes before serving**:  
   - Boil the potatoes until soft.  
   - Mash with butter and a splash of milk.  
3. **12 minutes before serving**:  
   - Unroll the puff pastry and bake on its included paper sheet until golden.  
4. Serve the stew with mashed potatoes and flaky pastry on the side.  
   Add a spoonful of **Bisto gravy granules** to the mash or stew for extra richness.

---

## Leftover Tip

Once cooled, transfer leftover stew into **glass Tupperware** and refrigerate.  
It’s even tastier the next day!
